Course Curriculum

GST(Goods and Service Tax)

  • Filing of Tax Returns
  • GST (Goods and Service Tax)
  • SGST, CGST and IGST
  • GST Records and Accounts to be maintained
  • Registers to be maintained – GSTR 1, GSTR 2, GSTR 3
  • Filing of Returns on the GST portals
  • Refund and Exemptions
  • Penalties

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

  • TDS Computation
  • TDS Receivable and Payable
  • TDS Rates, E-Payment of Challan 281
  • Filing TDS Return Form 26Q, Checking 26AS, Procedure of Form 15CB/15CA and 27Q
  • Issue of TDS Certificates
  • Concept of 15G/15H Form
  • Certificate of Lower Deduction
  • Issue of TAN No
  • TDS Late payment Interest and Penalty

Income Tax

  • Income Tax Computation and Filing of ITR For Salaried Person
  • Advance Tax Computation and Deposit
  • E-Filing ITR for Small Proprietorship
  • Partnership Business
  • Practical implications of Different Incomes
  • Allowed and Disallowed Expenses
  • Understanding Expense Disallowed
  • Current Tax and Deferred Tax Computation for Companies
  • Challan 280 Online and Offline Procedure
  • ITR1 – ITR 6 filing of returns on the E-Filing Portal
