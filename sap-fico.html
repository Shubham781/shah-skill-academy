Course Description

SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products) is one of the most widely used Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions globally. It helps businesses streamline operations by integrating various departments into a centralized system. With SAP, every department—whether finance, HR, sales, or supply chain—can access and share real-time data, leading to improved collaboration, decision-making, and productivity.

What is SAP FICO?

SAP FICO (Financial Accounting and Controlling) is a core functional module in SAP ERP that enables organizations to manage their entire financial lifecycle. This module plays a vital role in recording, processing, and analyzing financial transactions, making it essential for companies aiming for financial transparency and compliance.

Our SAP FICO course covers all major components needed to master this module, including:

  • Financial Accounting (FI) – Handles external reporting such as balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and tax accounting.
  • Controlling (CO) – Focuses on internal reporting and cost tracking to support business decision-making.

With SAP FICO, you will learn how to manage data related to:

  • Sales Orders
  • Shipping and Delivery
  • Billing and Invoicing
  • General Ledger Accounting
  • Asset Accounting
  • Cost Center Accounting
  • Profitability Analysis

This course is ideal for finance professionals, accounting specialists, and ERP users who want to enhance their skills and gain in-demand SAP certification. Whether you're a beginner or looking to upskill, our hands-on training will prepare you to handle real-world financial scenarios using SAP.

Get ready to boost your career in SAP Finance with industry-relevant knowledge and practical expertise.
