What is Tally ERP 9?

Tally ERP 9 is a powerful tool that helps businesses manage their financial and inventory processes efficiently. It offers features like ledger creation, GST implementation, payroll management, and bank reconciliation, making it an essential tool for accountants and business owners.

Our Tally ERP 9 course covers all major components needed to master this software, including:

  • Financial Accounting Principles – Learn the basics of accounting and how to apply them in Tally ERP 9.
  • Inventory Management – Manage stock, create unit measurements, and track inventory efficiently.
  • GST Compliance – Understand and implement GST in your business operations.
  • Payroll Management – Handle employee salaries, deductions, and compliance with ease.

With Tally ERP 9, you will learn how to manage data related to:
